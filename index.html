About Me

Amid soaring healthcare expenses and the pressing need for effective patient data management, the healthcare industry faces unprecedented challenges.
In fact, inefficient data management is estimated to cost the healthcare system billions annually.
By 2025, healthcare data is projected to surpass 2,314 exabytes, highlighting the critical role of big data.
Nancy's goal is to be at the forefront of addressing these issues by harnessing the power of big data to introduce groundbreaking healthcare data solutions.
Her aim is to ensure secure patient data management, promising to revolutionize diagnostics and significantly improve patient outcomes.
